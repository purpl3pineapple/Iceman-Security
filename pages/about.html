About Iceman

I'm Dayon, a former semi-professional football player turned founder of Iceman Security, a company born from my deep-seated commitment to protecting others. My life's journey has been a tumultuous one, marked by encounters with the darker side of existence, including periods of incarceration that have sharpened my resolve and fortified my ability to navigate challenging situations. Through my experiences on the gridiron, brushes with nefarious characters, and collaborations with law enforcement, I've gained invaluable insights into negotiating complexities and diffusing confrontations.

Recognizing the scarcity of those willing to confront adversity head-on to ensure peace, I've embraced my role as a guardian of innocence with unwavering pride. I launched Iceman Security over a decade ago, driven by a profound love for what I do and a relentless determination to make a positive impact. Beyond the professional realm, I find solace in physical fitness, cherish moments with my family, and maintain a steadfast loyalty to the Dallas Cowboys. Each day presents a new opportunity to leverage my skills and experiences in service of a safer, more secure environment for all.
